## Casting Insights

### Seed-2.0-pro for Cross-Cultural Reasoning
- Successfully adopted each language's cognitive framework
- Didn't just translate — actually *thought within* the constraint system
- 50/50 on Chinese safe navigation: perfect score
- **Best for:** Tasks requiring perspective-shifting, cultural sensitivity, creative problem-solving
- **Cost:** ~$0.05-0.10 per experiment via DeepInfra

### Why Not Other Models?
- DeepSeek has English bias in training data
- GLM models have Chinese bias (great for Chinese experiments, bad for Navajo)
- Seed-2.0-pro was the most culturally neutral model available

### Universal Concepts (emerged in ALL 3 traditions)
1. Process > Nouns
2. Future = Hidden Present
3. Midwife Posture
4. Conflict = Misperception

These weren't primed — they emerged independently. The convergence across orthogonal linguistic systems suggests these are structural, not cultural.
