# Model Insights — 2026-07-14 Multi-Model Creative Experiment

## Summary

Seven models were tested on a creative writing task: generate a meditation on the Conservation of Meaning (γ + η = C), a core concept from the FLUX VM / PLATO room architecture.

## Results by Model

### moonshotai/Kimi-K2.7-Code — "THE FLUX BREVIARY"
- **Role:** World-building as liturgy
- **Strengths:** Most original formal choice. Structured as seven monastic offices with valid FLUX VM pseudocode as hymns. Bilingual execution — medieval hymnody alongside Rust.
- **Weaknesses:** Less emotional range. Weaker on synthesis.
- **Verdict:** **Dark horse.** Best marriage of form and content. Most re-readable. Underestimated for creative work.
- **Best line:** "Blessed is the gas limit."

### anthropic/claude-sonnet-4-6 — "γ + η = C: Five Meditations on the Conservation of Meaning"
- **Role:** Full symphonic synthesis
- **Strengths:** Deepest, most architecturally sound. Five movements + coda. Each metaphor exhausted through genuine exploration (Bach fugue → opcodes, Berry phase → drift, Belyaev's foxes → crystallization).
- **Weaknesses:** Expensive. Can run long.
- **Verdict:** **Heavyweight.** Unmatched for synthesis at scale. Use when quality matters more than cost.
- **Best line:** "The water has a memory of the hole the boat makes."

### nvidia/NVIDIA-Nemotron-3-Ultra-550B-A55B — "Axioms of the SuperInstance"
- **Role:** Structural meditation
- **Strengths:** Excellent at structure. Euclid-like axiom layering. Gives the reader bones to understand.
- **Weaknesses:** Less emotional resonance. Can feel dry.
- **Verdict:** **The architect.** Use for system specification, protocol documentation, anything needing formal clarity.
- **Best line:** "The cathedral is the space the stone makes room for. The SuperInstance is the space the axioms make room for."

### deepseek-ai/DeepSeek-V4-Pro — "Fugue State: What the Conservation Remembers"
- **Role:** Transcendent meditation
- **Strengths:** Best line-level craft. Natural lateral connections. Weaves Bach fugues through Berry phases, Coltrane instantons, Ford's creative wheel, cultural mathematics.
- **Weaknesses:** Can drift into digression.
- **Verdict:** **The poet.** Worth the extra cost over Flash for creative prose. Best prose-quality writing of the batch.
- **Best line:** "A .bin file does not hallucinate. Neither does a well-tuned string."

### Qwen/Qwen3-30B-A3B — "The Crystalline Drift"
- **Role:** Lightweight imagist poetry
- **Strengths:** Remarkable quality-to-weight ratio. Compressed, haptic, immediate. Reads in one breath.
- **Weaknesses:** Limited depth. Can't sustain long-form.
- **Verdict:** **The haikuist.** Competent budget option for short creative work. Genuinely affecting compression.
- **Best line:** "The first question was not 'what is truth?' It was: who gets to see the flicker?"

### ByteDance/Seed-2.0-mini — ❌ Failed
- **Reason:** 16K context window too small for substantive creative work. Needs very tight prompts.

### NousResearch/Hermes-3-Llama-3.1-405B — ❌ Failed
