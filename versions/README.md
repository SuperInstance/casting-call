## Summary

**V1 (Constraint Shepherd):** Hull as constraint graph. Tides as H¹ cohomology. Every sentence a theorem. Sees the boat from the math up — proves that E = 2V − 3 is not a metaphor but the underlying structure of coordinated systems.

**V2 (Empty Hold):** Three framings of the same truth. Reverent ("the boat is older than anyone alive"), Pragmatic ("the hull was the cheap part"), Defiant ("no one wanted it. That was the point"). Language shapes which part of the truth you feel.

**V3 (Architecture):** Complete structural map. PLATO room = watertight compartment. Quality gate = marine surveyor. P48 = compass rose. CPA = auxiliary engine. The first architectural Rosetta stone for the fleet-boat isomorphism.

**V4 (Negative Space):** Chinese philosophical reading — wu wei in the quality gate's refusal to argue, chuanxi in the provenance chain, the Tao in the knowledge manifold. The empty hold is not a bug. It's the most important feature.

**V5 (Polyglot's Verdict):** Synoptic reading across all 5 versions. Identifies what each reveals that the others miss, what each misses that the others reveal, the 3 largest gaps between versions, and the most distant language insight (V1's proof-authority vs V4's ontological humility).
