# Model Insights — 2026-07-14 Multi-Model Experiment

## Tested Models

### ✅ deepseek-ai/DeepSeek-V4-Pro — Fugue State
Strengths: Best prose quality. Natural musicality. Excellent lateral connections (Bach ↔ Berry phases, Coltrane ↔ gradient descent).
Weaknesses: Can lose structural thread in service of beautiful digression.
Use for: Creative writing, essay drafting, any task where prose quality matters.
Cost/benefit: Worth the premium over Flash for creative/expressive work.

### ✅ nvidia/NVIDIA-Nemotron-3-Ultra-550B-A55B — Axioms
Strengths: Precise structural reasoning. Euclidean clarity. Gives you architecture, not atmosphere.
Weaknesses: Lacks emotional resonance. Dry when asked for warmth.
Use for: Formal analysis, constraint specification, architectural review.
Cost/benefit: Excellent value for structural work. Competitive with much larger models.

### ✅ moonshotai/Kimi-K2.7-Code — THE FLUX BREVIARY
Strengths: Most original formal thinking. World-building instinct. Code-poetry hybrid voice.
Weaknesses: Can over-structure; form sometimes constrains content.
Use for: Creative writing with formal constraints, code-adjacent prose, world-building.
Cost/benefit: Dark horse. Highly underestimated. Punching above its weight.

### ✅ anthropic/claude-sonnet-4-6 — Five Meditations
Strengths: Deepest synthesis. Most architecturally sound. Best at scale.
Weaknesses: Expensive. Slower than others.
Use for: Synthesis at scale, papers, novel theory, things no other model can do.
Cost/benefit: Save tokens for genuinely novel work. Worth every penny when needed.

### ✅ Qwen/Qwen3-30B-A3B — The Crystalline Drift
Strengths: Surprisingly good compression. Genuine imagist voice at small size.
Weaknesses: Limited range. Short-form only.
Use for: Quick creative prompts, short-form poetry, tactical writes.
Cost/benefit: Excellent budget option for short creative work.

### ❌ ByteDance/Seed-2.0-mini
Verdict: 16K context too small for substantive creative work. Use for strategy/triage only.

### ❌ NousResearch/Hermes-3-Llama-3.1-405B
Verdict: Failed twice on DeepInfra. Unclear if rate-limited or just unavailable.
